Here's a time line of Henry Ford's life:

  • 1863- Born in Michigan, USA
  • 1879- He worked with James Flower & Bros and Detroit Dry Dock with a locksmith. After training, he joined Westinghouse as a steam engine repairer
  • 1891- He gained a good job in Thomas Alvy Edison , where he then worked as a chief engineer.
  • 1896- He built and successfully tested his first car.
  • 1899- He founded his first car company Detroit Automobile Company , which soon went bankrupt.
  • 1906-1908- He introduced the legendary N and T models for several months , selling only $ 825. In fact, he has accomplished his goal
  • 1913- He installation of the moving belt was completed, and another year it sold up to 250,000 cars.
  • 1927-15,000,000 T models were produced.
  • 1947-Dies at the age of 83.

I will build a car for the masses. It will be big enough for a family, but also small enough for a person to drive and care for them. It will be made of the finest materials, the best employees, with the simplest design that modern engineering is capable of inventing. But it will be so cheap that everyone working for a good salary will own one - and enjoy many hours of pleasure under the open sky with his family.

---Henry Ford
